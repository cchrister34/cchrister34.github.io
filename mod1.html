About the project

The diving Argentavis mod was created as a personal project aiming to improve the experience of using the Argentavis in Ark Survival Ascended. The mod adds diving mechanics to the Argentavis, allowing it to dive towards the ground at high speeds and then pull up to gain altitude again. This balances the Argentavis with other flyers in the game, as it is one of the oldest creatures within the game and has not received updates to its flying mechanics meaning that it was easily outperformed by newer flyers added in later updates. This mod was created using the official Ark Survival Devkit within Unreal Engine 5.5.

What I learned

This was my first experience working inside a large development kit and modding an existing game, and it was a valuable learning experience about how AAA studios design and implement game mechanics. It was very simple for me to rewire existing mechanics to create the diving behaviour due to the architecture of the devkit, which was designed to be modular and flexible to allow for easy modding. I also learned about the importance of balancing new mechanics with existing ones to ensure that they fit well within the game and provide a fun and engaging experience for players.



Problems Faced

One of the key challenges I faced during this project ensuring that the mechanics applied to the creature fit within the base game. Making the extension feel like a natural part of the creature's existing mechanics was important to me, and I had to ensure that the diving behaviour was balanced with the existing flying mechanics to avoid making the creature overpowered or underpowered compared to other flyers in the game. I also listened to feedback provided from players who suggested improvements to the dive animation to look more natural as seen here Reddit Post



Conclusion

Overall, this project was a valuable learning experience that not only imrpoved my playing experience of Ark Survival Ascended but also taught me about game design, modding, and the importance of balancing new mechanics with existing ones. It was rewarding to see the positive feedback from players who enjoyed the new diving mechanics and felt that it improved their experience of using the Argentavis in the game.
